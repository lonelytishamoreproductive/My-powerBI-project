# My-powerBI-project
Project Title:Global Sales & Product Portfolio Performance

-Short Description:A Power BI solution for Sales and Marketing teams to track and analyze sales volume, revenue trends, and product-specific performance across different geographical regions. The dashboard focuses on identifying top-performing markets and product contribution to overall revenue.

-Tech Stack Analysis & Visualization: Microsoft Power BI Desktop/Service (Used for creating the visuals and layout shown in the image) 

-Data Preparation: Power Query (M Language) (For connecting to sales data and cleaning/shaping it) 
-Data Modeling & Measures: DAX (For calculating aggregates like Sum of Boxes Shipped and Sum of Amount, and year-over-year changes).
-Data SourceSales Transaction Database (e.g., SQL Server, Azure Synapse, or data warehouse extracts)  CRM Data (Customer and Country dimensions)  Product Master Data (Product names and categories)Features/Highlights- Geographic Sales Ranking: Ranking of sales volume by Country (e.g., Australia, Canada, UK, India, USA). 

--Product Performance Breakdown: Visualization of total boxes shipped per individual product (e.g., Smooth, Ecarl, Caramel). 

Waterfall Analysis: Decomposition of the Sum of Amount (Revenue) showing increases and decreases attributed to various products and periods.  - Time Series Trend: Tracking the Sum of Amount over time (Year, Quarter, Month) to spot seasonality or major shifts.

-Key Question:How is our sales revenue performing over time, and which products/countries are the primary drivers (or detriments) to our financial goals? 

-Business Impact and Insights- Targeted Marketing Investment: Identify top countries/products to focus marketing spend for maximum ROI.  
Inventory/Production Planning: Optimize stock levels and manufacturing schedules based on sales volume trends per product.
Financial Forecasting: Use the time-series data to predict future revenue and sales volume. 
Product Strategy: Quickly identify underperforming products for potential discontinuation or re-evaluation, as demonstrated by the Waterfall chart.
